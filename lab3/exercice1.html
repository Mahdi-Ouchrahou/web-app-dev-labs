30/03/2023: 7th day of Ramadan

Today was the 7th day of ramadan, I had iftar together with some of my friends and we prepared some Moroccan food.

Did you know? Ramadan is the ninth month of the Islamic calendar, and it is considered to be the holiest month for Muslims. During Ramadan, Muslims fast from dawn until sunset, abstaining from food, drink, smoking, and other physical needs. Fasting is one of the Five Pillars of Islam, and it is a way for Muslims to purify their souls and practice self-discipline, gratitude, and charity.
